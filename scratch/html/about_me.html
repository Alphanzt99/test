A History of Devotion

Since the beginning...

I've always loved ice cream. My Dad and I would bond over ice cream all the time when I was a kid, and we would frequently eat it late at night together. I wasn't always sure of our coexistance, however.

I used to think I'd get in trouble whenever he walked in, but I quickly learned that if I:

  1. Scooted over
  2. Offered him a
    • fresh, clean spoon
    • the lion's share of the tub
  3. And made pleasant conversation

we could peacefully coexist. It was there that I realized that my Dad and I both shared a common love for ice cream. My Mom eventually found out and would always tell me "You're going to get fat if you don't cut that out!" Once I started to get older, I began to see the possibility of me looking like this.

So, I decided to stop eating ice cream so late at night, and so often. But, my love for it has never ceased, and perahps one day I'll go back to my old ways.
